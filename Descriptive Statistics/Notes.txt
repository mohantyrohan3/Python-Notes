
1.  Mean
    X̄ = (Sum of values ÷ Number of values)

    X̄ = (x1 + x2 + x3 +….+xn)/n
    
    Mean of Population (u (meun))
    Mean of sample (x bar)
    


Symmetric Data - Majority of data points lie near mean

Left Skewed Data - Mean < Median < Mode  ( In graph it has a longer left tail)

Right Skewed Data - Mean > Median > Mode  ( In graph it has a longer right tail)
